Exchange & Return Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. Read our exchange and return guidelines below.

1. Eligibility for Returns & Exchanges

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • Products must be in their original packaging, unworn, and with tags attached.
  • Proof of purchase (order ID or receipt) is required.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Customized or personalized items.
  • Clearance sale products marked as "Final Sale".
  • Used, washed, or damaged items.

3. How to Initiate a Return

  1. Go to the Track Order section in your account.
  2. Select the item you want to return and click "Request Return".
  3. Print the return label and securely pack the item.
  4. Drop the package at the nearest courier service.
  5. Once received, our team will inspect the item and process your refund or exchange.

4. Refund Processing

Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after approval. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

5. Exchange Policy

If you wish to exchange an item for a different size or color, follow the return process and place a new order for the desired item.
